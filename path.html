Path

Finding your way in today's traditional educational institutions can be quite confusing and even arduous and misleading when it comes to career options in today's job market.

Here at Hacker: University you will be diving into a well-rounded guidance across 4 crucial foundations for a solid technology career.

Design

Create visual concepts to communicate ideas that inspire, inform, or captivate consumers. They develop the overall layout and production design for advertisements, brochures, magazines, and corporate reports.

Development

Write code. Through the use of computer languages to create instructions for computers to perform and follow. Work closely with others to develop, maintain, debug, and test programs.

Data

Translate numbers into plain English Every business collects data, whether it's sales figures, market research, logistics, or transportation costs. Organize data and use it to help make better decisions.

Security

Protect data and information from attacks. Planning and implementing security measures to protect computer systems, networks and data.
